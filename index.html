Gigster

Design | Front-End Development (HTML, CSS, Javascript)

Product:

Gigster is an on-demand software development platform.

Project Details:

Product Managers and Developers need to quickly see the status of their project on two primary metrics: timeliness and customer satisfaction. We created a visual interface using a progress bar for timeliness and an emoticon face for customer satisfaction. They have potential states of all's good (green), be careful (orange), and this is going south quickly (red) to notify users when action is necessary.

Gamesmith

Design

Product:

Gamesmith is the authoritative who's who of video game professionals.

Project Details:

We wanted to combine the clean feel of a networking site with the modern playfulness of a game. The primary mechanism is a deck of cards containing other professionals in your network. Each user has a card which lists their accomplishments, and users can validate or invalidate other user's accomplishments. I created a color palette based on video game imagery, and I combined clean typography with modern iconography to give the content heavy product a lighter feel.

Coinmotion

Design | Development (HTML, CSS, Javascript)

Product:

Bitcoin. Easy, fast, the way it should be.

Project Details:

We had two goals: 1.) make a large amount of textual information more visual, and 2.) increase engagement with the site. We wanted to take the many possible actions/views on the primary wallet interface and consolidate them into a single screen view. We did this by giving send money (one of the highest use cases) a permanent home at the top and moving less common transactional flows into modals. We also added in light gamification of the registration system to increase the amount of information provided.

iTripSmart

Design

Product:

Intelligent personalized trip planning.

Project Details:

We wanted to create a single page experience for road trip planning. We did this by implementing a navigation pane on the left side and an information pane on the right side. Within these users can select their interest preferences, view information on potential stops, and add or remove stops. All trip information is saved in their profile, along with pictures from their trip, to create a visual record of their adventures.

Riskmaster

Design | Development (Obj-C)

Product:

A visual portfolio management tool.

Project Details:

Tim Draper asked me to create an application for him to easily monitor and view information on the investments he's made from his personal funds. We decided to make a no frills interface - clean, simple, and easily readable. There were two primary use cases we designed for: a.) the ability to swipe through all investments in a card-like interface, and b.) the ability to quickly skim through companies by category or fund. The card back also contained information on the CEO and current stock performance (not pictured).
